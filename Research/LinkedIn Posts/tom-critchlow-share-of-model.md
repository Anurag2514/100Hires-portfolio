## Why This Source Matters

This post introduces the concept of "Share of Model," a framework for understanding how AI systems describe, compare, and recommend brands. Tom argues that AI search should be viewed as a brand perception challenge rather than a ranking challenge.

## Main Thesis

AI search is changing marketing from a visibility challenge to a brand perception challenge. Tom Critchlow argues that companies must understand how AI models describe, compare, and recommend their brands, not just how often they appear in search results.

## Five Key Arguments

### 1. AI Models Speak on Behalf of Brands

AI systems increasingly act as intermediaries between businesses and customers, influencing how brands are perceived before users visit a website.

### 2. Share of Model Is Becoming a Critical Metric

Marketers need to understand how often and in what context AI systems mention their brands within generated responses.

### 3. Brand Messaging Must Be Reflected in AI Answers

Organizations should ensure that their core value propositions and brand claims are accurately represented in AI-generated content.

### 4. AI Search Requires New Marketing Investments

Brands may need to rethink budgets, channels, and content strategies to influence the information AI systems learn from.

### 5. AI Content Production Requires Strategic Oversight

AI-assisted content workflows can improve efficiency, but successful implementation requires governance, quality control, and alignment with brand positioning.

## What Is Share of Model?

Share of Model measures how frequently and favorably a brand appears in AI-generated responses compared to competitors. It focuses on AI recommendations and brand representation rather than search rankings.

## Why Is It Replacing Share of Search?

Traditional Share of Search measures visibility in search engine results, while Share of Model evaluates visibility within AI-generated answers where users increasingly discover and compare brands.

## How Do AI Systems Influence Brand Perception?

AI systems summarize, compare, and recommend brands, shaping user perceptions before direct interaction occurs. Their responses can influence trust, preference, and purchase consideration.

## Content Production Recommendations

* Create content that clearly communicates brand expertise and value propositions.
* Publish authoritative content that reinforces key brand messages.
* Maintain consistency across all content assets and channels.
* Focus on content that can be easily interpreted and referenced by AI systems.

## AI Workflow Recommendations

* Integrate AI tools into content production workflows while maintaining human oversight.
* Develop processes for reviewing AI-generated outputs against brand standards.
* Use AI to improve efficiency without sacrificing accuracy or strategic alignment.
* Continuously test and refine AI-assisted content operations.

## Actionable Takeaways for B2B SaaS Companies

1. Monitor how AI systems describe your products and services.
2. Strengthen thought leadership and expertise-driven content.
3. Ensure key differentiators are consistently reflected across digital assets.
4. Invest in both AI search strategy and experimentation initiatives.
5. Track brand mentions, recommendations, and sentiment within AI-generated answers.
